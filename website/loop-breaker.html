How It Works

Load. Select. Let go.

Loop Breaker's modifier engine does the creative heavy lifting. You decide the boundaries — it fills in the surprises.

Load Your Loops

Drag WAV, AIFF, FLAC, or MP3 files onto the 8-pad grid. Each pad loops its sample in sync with your DAW's transport and tempo.

Select Target Pads

Click or MIDI-trigger pads to mark them as targets. If you don't select any, the engine picks 1–4 pads for you automatically.

Modifiers Fire Automatically

Every N bars (you choose 1–16), a randomly selected modifier transforms your targeted pads — effects, slicing, pitch shifts, speed changes, and more.

Shape the Probabilities

Use the Probability tab to dial in which modifiers appear and how often. Automate them from your DAW or map them to MIDI CC hardware.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for live performance, sound design sessions, and creative exploration.

🎛

Automatic Modifier Engine

24 modifier types across four categories — buffer transforms, channel effects, master effects, and specials — applied on a beat-synced timer you control.

🎲

Probability Control

Per-modifier and per-pad probability sliders let you shape the randomness. Set a modifier to zero to disable it entirely, or crank it up for chaos.

✂️

Beat Slicing & Glitch

Beat Slice, Arp Slice, and Slice Repeater chop your loops into rhythmic subdivisions and reassemble them in randomized or stuttered patterns.

🔊

Multi-Output Routing

8 independent stereo output buses plus a master mix. Route each pad to its own DAW mixer channel for independent processing and recording.

🎹

Full MIDI Integration

Map pads, the modifier toggle, and preset slots to MIDI notes. Map probability sliders to MIDI CC. Compatible with Akai MPD, Maschine, Launchpad, and more.

💾

Modifier Presets (A–D)

Snapshot the complete modifier state across all 8 pads into four preset slots. Recall them instantly via click or MIDI for scene-based performances.

⏱

Time-Stretch & Pitch-Shift

Real-time SoundTouch-powered time-stretching and pitch-shifting. Change speed without affecting pitch, or shift pitch independently.

🧩

Parts System

Divide each loop into 1–4 equal sections (A–D) for structured arrangements. The Switch Part modifier moves between sections automatically.

🎨

11 Visual Themes

From Neon Rave to Warm Paper — pick a theme that fits your studio vibe. Smooth crossfade transitions between themes.

DAW Integration

Fits right into your workflow

Loop Breaker follows your DAW's transport and tempo automatically. Every parameter that matters — master volume, all probability sliders, pad target weights — is exposed for host automation. Draw curves, use LFOs, or control everything from hardware.

Hands-on MIDI mapping

Loop Breaker is designed to be played, not just watched. Map the 8 pads to your drum controller's note grid for tactile pad selection. Assign the modifier toggle and preset recall slots to dedicated buttons. Map probability sliders to CC faders for live probability sculpting.

MIDI Learn is everywhere — Shift+Click or right-click any assignable control, play a note or move a knob, and you're mapped. Assignments persist across DAW sessions.
